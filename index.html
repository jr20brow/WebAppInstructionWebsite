How To Take A Screenshot

The Basics

Depending on the type of computer you're using, you can simply do a combination of keyboard shortcuts to take a screenshot. Please follow the instructions below for Mac/Apple system:

  1. First hold Ctrl to intiate the command.
  2. While holding Ctrl also hold Shift
  3. Finally, press 3, and you have taken a screenshot of your whole screen.

Advanced Techniques

There are other ways to take screenshots. You could also use, different number buttons to do different types of screenshots. For example, if you use 4, you can drag out and capture a specific portion of your screen. Learn more here.
